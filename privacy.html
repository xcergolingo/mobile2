GoLingo Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how and why we collect it, how long we store your information and who has access to it. We may change this policy from time to time so please check this page occasionally. By using our website and apps, you're agreeing to be bound by this policy.
Any questions regarding this policy and our privacy practices should be sent to support@golingo.mobi

Who are we?

GoLingo is an app to help people to learn languages together For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, GoLingo or we means xCers Inc, the developer of GoLingo.

What information is collected, how and why?

We do store or have access to the data (the contents and translations etc.) and share the data with other users/learners so other users like you can benefit from your data. When you contact us via e-mail and/or the feedback button in the GoLingo application:

We store your e-mail address, the e-mail and any additional information you provided. We use this information to answer your e-mail and/or to improve our website and apps.

Apple™s App Store¨ (GoLingo for iPhone/iPad and GoLingo for Mac)

Apple does not share your customer records or order details with us.

Google™s Play Store (GoLingo for Android)

Google™s Play Store grants us read-only access to your customer records. Your records are not stored by us. We do not have access to your payment details. GoLingo for Android requires permissions, click here to see what permissions and why.

How long is your information stored?

We will hold your personal information on our systems for as long as is necessary for the relevant activity.

Who has access to your information?

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your choices

We will not contact you for marketing purposes.

How you can access and update your information

You can review and correct the information that we hold about you. Please email us at support@golingo.mobi

Security precautions in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of your information

When you give us personal information, we take steps to ensure that it's treated securely. Non-sensitive details (your email address etc.) are transmitted normally over the internet, and this can never be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your information, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems.

Complaints
